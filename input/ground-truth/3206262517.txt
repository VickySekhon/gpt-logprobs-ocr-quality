SUPPOSED MURDER. —The body of an old woman has been found in a shed in a
brickyard at Ewyas Harold, Herefordshire, under circumstances leading to
the supposition that she had been murdered. The deceased had gone to the
shed to see if there were any tramps there, and as she did not return
search was made, and she was found lying in a pool of blood. Two tramps
have been arrested on suspicion.

FIRING A STACKYARD. —A boy named Bandy, aged 10 years, living at Wing,
near Leighton Buzzard, was remanded on Tuesday on a charge of setting
fire to the rick-yard of his master, Mr. W. Woolhead, on Monday evening.
Two very large wheat ricks and a stack of straw were destroyed. It is
alleged that the boy took a match from home, and, out of mischief, set
the stackyard in a blaze. The damage done amounts to 500l.
