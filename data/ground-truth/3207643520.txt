The Court of King's Bench, and the Court of Common Pleas, will adjourn
this day, till Easter term. The Court of Exchequer adjourned yesterday.
The business in the Court of King's Bench, standing over till next term,
consist of only eighteen causes, nine in Middlesex, and nine in London. In
the Court of Common Pleas there is an arrear of seven special jury
causes in Middlesex, and twenty special and common jury causes in
London.
