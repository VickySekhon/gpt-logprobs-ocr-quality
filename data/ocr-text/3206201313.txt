. ATTEMPT TO BUBKE A FEMALE.,YARMOUTH, SATURDAY. -A horrible attempt to murder took place in the vicinity of this town late on Thursday evening. The wretched victim of this diabolical act is a servant girl, named Mary Ann Proudfood, whose recovery is at present doubtful, and the party arrested on suspicion of having made the atrocious attempt is a corn porter, of the name of Samuel Howth, who was in the same service as the unfortunate girl, and by whom she is pregnant.
